Abhayam Prardhana Sanghamam

'ABHAYAM' is the prayer gahering,held on the first wednesday of every month. it commences at 10:30 am with songs and hymns by the parish choir. Believers from different walks of life participate in this meeting and return with a very blessed experience. Devotional talks and supplication in he mediation of st.mary, the mother of god are the soo thing experiences of every soul . lunch(nercha kanji) , given an offering,by faithful devotees is served to the gathering.free transportation facility isalso arranged for those who come from distant places.


Marian Puraskaram

'MARIAN PURESKARAM', a sign of recognition and appreciation of st mary's orthodox valliya pally, kallooppara to organisations,which carry out exemplary social services and philanthropic endeavours. It is awarded in connection with the feast of the ascension of st mary. the award is of rs 50001 and the certificate of appreciation


Diocese Choir Meeting

A meeting of the diocesan choir was held on August 9 at 10 am followed by Feast of St. Mary. The class was led by Fr. M P George, Professor of Malankara Orthodox Theological Seminary. Presence of our Vicars Fr. Koshy Philip, Fr. Lijo Mathew and former vicars Fr. Joji M Abraham, Fr. Jiji were blessing for the meeting.
