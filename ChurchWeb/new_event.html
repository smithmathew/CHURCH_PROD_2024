Events



Stone led feast

Every year in the 3rd day of malayala month karkidakam, church followed to celebrate kallitta feast, on that day evening procession and offering(neyappam) used to follow.

Parumala Perunnal

First Saturday of every November there will be evening prayer , followed by procession and dipashika.Youth used to conduct song service in name of " punyasmrithy" along with that meditation will be there. All the shrines in different part of the church used to have meditation message delivery , afterwards Sunday mornings holy mass will be there.

Shoonoyo Lent

Soonoyo lent is the mandatory one in connection with St.Mary's death and translation to paradise, remembered on August 1 to August 15. More than thousands of people are attending the feast services. Evening prayer and benedictory address were conducts in these 15 days. After the great Holy Service on 15th august, 'Puzhukkunercha will be given as a vow

Abhayam Prayer

'ABHAYAM' is the prayer gahering,held on the first wednesday of every month. it commences at 10:30 am with songs and hymns by the parish choir. Believers from different walks of life participate in this meeting and return with a very blessed experience.

Birth of holy Mary and 8days fasting

The birth of holy Mary is celebrated on September 1-8 days. On that days in church morning prayer will starts at 7am and afterwards meditation and Message delivery will be there. On 7th day followed by evening prayer there will be procession and 8th day morning followed by HolyMass there will be "pachornercha".

Marian Puraskaram

'MARIAN PURESKARAM', a sign of recognition and appreciation of st mary's orthodox valliya pally, kallooppara to organisations,which carry out exemplary social services and philanthropic endeavours. It is awarded in connection with the feast of the ascension of st mary. the award is of rs 50001 and the certificate of appreciation
